Digital Media Final Project

The Exodus Initiative

Exodus is an immersive narrative event where students play as delegates from fictional nations trying to escape earth and find a new place in the galaxy to call home. Along the way, the game encourages critical and creative thinking in order to achieve the best results. Game elements are communicated through various digital mediums including film, graphic design, and video games.
